Brizo's Promise to our Customers

All our food is sustainably sourced and locally sourced when possible. It is one of our top priorities to make sure that the fisherman that we purchase our food from practice sustainable fishing, and the fish farmers practice clean and sustainable farming.

We only use organic products at Brizo’s.

While Evanston is far away from any seas, we do utilize the local fisherman here in Illinois seasonally for freshwater fish, and we get our produce from Illinois farmers.
